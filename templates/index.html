How to use Sitations

  1. 1. Make sure your bookmarks toolbar is visible.
  2. Safari: 'View' > 'Show Bookmarks Bar'
  3. Chrome: 'View' > 'Always Show Bookmarks Bar'
  4. Safari: 'View' > 'Toolbars' > 'Bookmarks Toolbar'
  5. 2. Drag the Sitations logo into your bookmarks bar.
  6. 3. Click the bookmark to use.
